About Us

Trusted By 200 + satisfied clients

Welcome to Dream Hub Pvt. Ltd., a dynamic and innovative media company and production house nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, Ward No. 15. At Dream Hub, we specialize in capturing the essence of moments through our comprehensive range of services, making us your one-stop destination for all things media.

Photo And Video Production

Photography is an art form that we master, whether it's immortalizing your special day with enchanting wedding shoots or creating stunning visuals that tell a story. Our adept team of photographers brings a unique perspective to every project, ensuring that each image resonates with authenticity and emotion.


In the realm of videography, Dream Hub takes storytelling to new heights. From cinematic masterpieces to compelling documentaries, we leverage state-of-the-art equipment and a creative approach to craft videos that leave a lasting impact. Whether it's a corporate project, a commercial, or a personal celebration, our team is dedicated to transforming ideas into captivating visual narratives.


Dream Hub excels in TVC (Television Commercial) production, bringing your brand to life through visually striking and conceptually powerful advertisements. We understand the power of visual communication and strive to create commercials that not only grab attention but also convey your message effectively.


Ads are not limited to television; our expertise extends to digital platforms. From social media campaigns to online advertising, we tailor our services to meet the demands of the ever-evolving digital landscape. Our team is adept at creating content that resonates with your target audience and maximizes your online presence.


In the realm of graphics designing, Dream Hub stands out for its creativity and attention to detail. Whether you need a captivating logo, visually appealing marketing collateral, or striking branding materials, our graphic designers are committed to bringing your vision to life.


Located in Kathmandu's vibrant Ward No. 15, Dream Hub is not just a media company; it's a creative hub where dreams are transformed into visual realities. We are passionate about our craft, dedicated to our clients, and committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake. Join us on a journey where imagination knows no bounds, and let Dream Hub be the architect of your visual dreams.
